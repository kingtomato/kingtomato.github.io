Appendix I used to know what that meant...

HappyMod
Cheat written by HaPpY that replaced the flag with a very large model (with a happy face). Also enabled playes to see foe arrows through walls.
LastHope
Anti-cheating patch applied to tribes to prevent use of HappyMod, written by Andrew. More information can be found here.
